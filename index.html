Welcome To All Smiles on Riverside

Start with a Great Smile

All Smiles On Riverside, 1550 Riverside Ave , Ste B , Jacksonville , FL 32204, is a premier dental clinic dedicated to providing advanced family dental care. Dental care has been served to Jacksonville from this location over 25 years . Our team of experienced professionals is committed to ensuring that each of our patients leaves our clinic with a brighter, healthier smile.

Our Services

What We Provide

  • Dental Implants

    Long-lasting dental implants that offer a natural feel and appearance, perfect for replacing missing teeth

  • Oral Hygiene

    We take care of oral hygiene

  • Full Mouth Reconstruction

    A comprehensive restoration plan that rejuvenates your entire smile, tailored to meet your specific needs.

  • Emergency Dental Care

    Prompt, professional care for dental emergencies, including broken teeth, severe pain, and other urgent issues.

  • Dentures

    Custom-designed dentures to restore your smile and improve your ability to chew comfortably.

  • Crowns and Bridges

    High-quality crowns and bridges crafted to match the natural look and function of your teeth.

Our Services

Our Procedures

  • Pediatric Dentistry

    Specialized dental care for children, focusing on developing healthy habits and ensuring strong, cavity-free teeth in a comfortable environment.

  • Restorative Care

      Treatments like fillings, crowns, and implants designed to repair damaged teeth and restore their function, appearance, and health.

  • Orthodontics

    Corrective treatments such as braces or aligners to straighten teeth, improve alignment, and enhance oral health and aesthetics.

  • Gum Health

      Preventive and therapeutic care to maintain healthy gums, prevent gum disease, and support overall oral hygiene and well-being.
