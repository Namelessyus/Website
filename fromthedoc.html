All Smiles On Riverside, 1550 Riverside Ave, Ste B, Jacksonville, FL 32204, is a premier dental clinic dedicated to providing advanced family dental care. Dental care has been served to Jacksonville from this location for over 25 years. Our team of experienced professionals is committed to ensuring that each of our patients leaves our clinic with a brighter, healthier smile.

Message from Dr. Jasmine Shakya​

As the owner of All Smiles on Riverside, I am honored to continue the tradition of trust and exceptional care that has been established here. I graduated from the University of Minnesota Dental School and have had the privilege of practicing dentistry in Wichita, Kansas, and Albuquerque, New Mexico. As a wife and mother of two, I understand how important it is to create a comfortable and trustworthy environment for our patients. Our focus on patient comfort and personalized care is at the core of everything we do at All Smiles on Riverside.

Our mission is simple: to deliver exceptional dental care with a focus on comfort, trust, and individualized attention. We aim to provide a warm, friendly atmosphere where every patient feels valued and their dental health is our top priority. At All Smiles on Riverside, we’re dedicated to building lasting relationships through high-quality care, skill, experience, and compassion.
